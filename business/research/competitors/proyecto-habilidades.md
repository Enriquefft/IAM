# What it is
Free Android app from University of Valparaíso (Chile). Targets executive function in kids with ASD. Game-based recreation of home and school activities to reinforce planning, sequencing, organization. Sister app: Proyect@ Emociones (empathy).

# Pricing
- Free. Android. No ads.

# Features
- Mini-game scenarios recreating home and school routines.
- Planning and organization tasks.
- Difficulty progression.
- Spanish-first.

# Strengths
- LATAM-origin (Chile), Spanish-first.
- 7,000+ downloads in Spanish-speaking countries (per academic reports).
- Free.
- Targets executive function, a real intervention target underserved by AAC-only apps.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- Android only.
- Older app; visible in design choices and limited update cadence.
- Single-team (academic) — limited resources, no commercial roadmap.
- No data product.
- Limited content depth.
- Discoverability poor — found mostly via academic listings and lists of LATAM autism apps.

# Evidence base
- Developed under academic research umbrella; small-scale pilots reported.

# Geographic / language coverage
- Spanish.
- Google Play worldwide.
