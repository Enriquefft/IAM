# What it is
Chilean app, launched June 2024 by Melissa Muñoz Flández (a late-diagnosed autistic engineer in Concepción). Built by-and-for the autistic community. Bundles pictograms, autoregulation tools, daily emotion log, to-do list, situational mutism cards, adapted job board, legal information, autism guides. Won MIT Technology Review LATAM 35 Innovators 2024.

# Pricing
- Free. iOS and Android. Currently Chile-only distribution.

# Features
- Pictograms and visual support guides.
- Autoregulation techniques.
- Daily emotion + to-do log.
- Situational mutism cards (a card to show if speech is unavailable in a moment).
- Adapted-job board.
- Autism legal information (Chilean law).
- General autism information guides.

# Strengths
- Built by an autistic adult — design priorities reflect lived experience.
- LATAM-origin (Chile) Spanish-first.
- Free.
- Targets adult and parent users, a segment most apps neglect.
- Recognized by MIT Tech Review LATAM — credibility signal.
- Situational mutism card and adapted-job board are features no other app in the category includes.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- Chile-only currently — biggest constraint on growth.
- Single founder; sustainability and roadmap depend on grant cycles.
- Feature breadth is wide but each feature is shallow (brief guides, basic logs).
- Legal info is Chile-specific — not portable to other LATAM markets.
- No clinical evidence base.
- No clinician dashboard.

# Evidence base
- None published.

# Geographic / language coverage
- Chilean Spanish.
- App Store + Play Store Chile.
