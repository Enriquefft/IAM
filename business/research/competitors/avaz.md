# What it is
India-origin AAC app from Avaz Inc. Symbol-and-text AAC with SymbolStix library. Available worldwide; an India edition supports six Indian languages (Hindi, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Telugu, Marathi) plus Gujarati and Bengali personalization. Targets autism, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, aphasia.

# Pricing
- 14-day free trial, no credit card.
- Subscription: monthly, annual, lifetime tiers (specific prices not published; `Estimate:` annual ~USD 99-149 based on third-party listings).
- Avaz Lite — free reduced version.

# Features
- 40,000+ pictograms (SymbolStix).
- Pre-built page sets: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced.
- TTS voices.
- Picture import + recording.
- Eight Indian languages plus English.
- iOS and Android.

# Strengths
- Cross-platform (iOS + Android).
- Eight Indian languages — only major AAC product covering these.
- Subscription model lower cost than Proloquo2Go for first year.
- Avaz Lite removes adoption friction.
- Established install base in India and Indian diaspora.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- India-first; LATAM presence weak. Spanish UI exists but isn't a marketing priority.
- Subscription pricing opaque — published only behind app store listings.
- No clinical evidence base.
- No data analytics for parent or therapist.
- SymbolStix is licensed from n2y — costs flow through to subscription price.

# Evidence base
- Anecdotal case studies; no peer-reviewed RCT.

# Geographic / language coverage
- English + 8 Indian languages.
- Spanish UI in international edition.
- Worldwide via app stores.
