# What it is
Social skills curriculum app from Brighten Learning. 16 lessons + 30 interactive scenes, each animated to Hollywood-cartoon production standard. Designed for kids 5-12 with autism, ADHD, Asperger's. Mapped to California State Board of Education standards and Common Core.

# Pricing
- iPad app — USD 89.99 one-time.
- 50% volume discount on 20+ copies (school).
- Web platform separate subscription.

# Features
- 16 lessons covering self-management, attentive listening, conflict resolution, relationship management, critical thinking, verbal/non-verbal communication.
- Pre-assessment, progress monitoring, post-assessment data.
- Tiered scenes for different learners.
- Designed for multi-setting use: classroom, small group, 1:1, independent.
- Aligned to CCSS and CA standards.

# Strengths
- Production quality — animation rivals consumer kids' content.
- Built-in assessment data is unusual in this category.
- Standards alignment makes it school-budget-eligible.
- Single one-time price for substantive curriculum.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- iPad only.
- English only.
- 16 lessons is a fixed catalog — no new content cadence.
- USD 89.99 is steep for an individual parent.
- No ongoing feedback loop; kid uses the lessons, not a relationship product.
- No LATAM presence, no Spanish localization.
- Curriculum framing leans neurotypical-conformity in places — a critique from autism acceptance community.

# Evidence base
- Standards-aligned; no RCT of the app.

# Geographic / language coverage
- English.
- App Store worldwide.
