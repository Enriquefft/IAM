# What it is
Free Android app from atlanTTic research center at Universidade de Vigo (with support from Indra and Fundación Universia). Aimed at adults and older teens with autism in workplace settings: pictogram-based communication aid plus practice / self-evaluation of speech and writing using a natural-language-generation system. Stated goal: scaffold the user toward eventual abandonment of pictograms.

# Pricing
- Free. Android.

# Features
- Pictogram communicator for workplace contexts.
- NLG-based practice and self-evaluation for speech and writing.
- Adaptable to specific job environments.
- Spanish-first.

# Strengths
- Targets adult workplace use case — a demographic almost no other autism app addresses.
- Built around a real graduation goal (move beyond pictograms), not an indefinite dependency.
- Spanish-first, LATAM-relevant.
- Free.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- Android only.
- Niche audience (adults with autism in employment) is small absolute market in LATAM.
- Single research-team product; cadence of updates depends on grants.
- No clinical evidence published.
- No analytics.
- Discoverability weak — distributed via press coverage and academic networks.

# Evidence base
- None published.

# Geographic / language coverage

- Available worldwide via Google Play.
