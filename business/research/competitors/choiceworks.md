# What it is
iPad app from Bee Visual offering four "boards": Schedule (sequence of tasks), Waiting (visual countdown timer), Feelings (emotion identifier with calming strategies), and Feelings Scale (1-5 intensity). Probably the longest-running and most-recommended autism visual support app inside North American occupational therapy.

# Pricing
- Choiceworks Lite — free; one schedule, sample boards.
- Choiceworks Full — USD 14.99 one-time IAP.
- Choiceworks+ subscription — pricing not published; adds device sync, themes, library assistant.

# Features
- 4 board types: Schedule, Waiting, Feelings, Feelings Scale.
- 180+ preloaded images.
- Recorded audio per cell.
- Custom photos, custom audio.
- Attach video to any image.
- Multi-user profiles.
- Unlimited schedules.

# Strengths
- The "Feelings Scale" board is a well-thought-through emotion-regulation tool — pulls in calming strategies per intensity level. This is rare in this category.
- Affordable one-time price keeps it as a default purchase recommendation.
- Strong OT/clinician advocacy in US.
- Multi-board: rare to get schedule + emotion in one app at this price.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- iPad only.
- English UI and audio; Spanish localization not present. Spanish-speaking families have to record their own audio for every cell.
- No cloud sync in the base product; +subscription required.
- No tracking, no progress reports, no clinician analytics dashboard.
- No AAC features.
- UI feels circa-2014.
- Bee Visual is a small studio — no roadmap visibility, slow update cadence.

# Evidence base
- Used widely in OT practice; no published RCT specific to the app.

# Geographic / language coverage
- English UI.
- Sold via App Store worldwide; no localization.
