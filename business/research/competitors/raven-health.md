# What it is
ABA practice-management + data-collection platform, with explicit pricing models including a "free if you use our managed billing" tier — an aggressive go-to-market in a category dominated by opaque pricing.

# Pricing
- Data Collection Essentials — USD 29/user/month + USD 300 one-time implementation (up to 25 clients).
- Managed Billing Bundle — 5% of monthly claims paid (software is "free" net of billing fee).
- Self-Billing Package — 2% of monthly claims submitted.

# Features
- ABA data collection on iOS and Android.
- VB-MAPP assessment tracking.
- Customizable graphs and reports.
- AI-generated session summaries.
- Scheduling.
- Insurance billing and claim builders.
- HIPAA-aligned.

# Strengths
- The "free with managed billing" model breaks the entry-cost barrier for small clinics.
- Cross-platform mobile (iOS + Android — most competitors are iOS only).
- VB-MAPP integration is a specific clinical hook.
- Pricing transparency.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- US only.
- ABA-only.
- English only.
- Smaller user base than CentralReach.
- Managed-billing model concentrates revenue from billing fees; could be margin-pressured.

# Evidence base
- Tooling.
