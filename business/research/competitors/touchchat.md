# What it is
Symbol-based AAC iPad app from PRC-Saltillo (the dedicated AAC device manufacturer). Bundles WordPower vocabularies (Nuvi Caine's framework) for various motor and cognitive abilities. Often paired with PRC-Saltillo dedicated speech-generating devices for funding via US insurance.

# Pricing
- TouchChat HD — USD 149.99 one-time (App Store).
- TouchChat HD with WordPower bundle — USD 299.99 one-time.
- iShare 1-year 30 GB cloud backup — USD 239.99/year.
- AAC Language Lab subscription (training material) — USD 19.95/year.
- Add-on language packs / vocabulary expansions — USD 59.99 to USD 149.99.
- Discover (free trial) — 30-day risk-free.
- Annual fall sale typically halves prices to ~USD 149.99.

# Features
- WordPower vocabularies (20, 25, 42, 60, 80, 108, 140 location grids).
- VocabPC, MultiChat 15, myCore vocabularies.
- TTS in English (US, UK), Spanish (LatAm + Castilian), French, German, Italian.
- Word prediction.
- Phrase-based and core-word vocabularies side by side.
- iShare cloud sync for backup.
- Switch scanning and head tracking (via iOS).

# Strengths
- The clinical-funding default in the US: PRC-Saltillo dedicated devices are typically reimbursed by Medicaid / private insurance, and TouchChat is the software layer.
- Multiple vocabulary frameworks available in one app — SLPs can match to user's motor and language level.
- Solid TTS quality including LatAm Spanish (Acapela).

# Weaknesses / gaps
- iOS/iPadOS only.
- Pricing model is fragmented and confusing — base app, add-ons, subscriptions for cloud and training stack up past USD 500.
- No data collection / progress tracking for therapists or parents — purely an output app.
- UI feels clinical and dated; setup is for professionals, not parents.
- Spanish localization exists but training material (AAC Language Lab) is overwhelmingly English-only.
- No LATAM distribution channel; insurance reimbursement does not exist in Peru / MX / CO / AR / EC / CL.
- No autism-specific scaffolding (emotions, schedule, social stories).

# Evidence base
- WordPower vocabulary backed by published clinical articles by Nuvi Caine et al.
- PRC-Saltillo publishes white papers, not RCTs of the software.

# Geographic / language coverage
- True L10n in EN, ES, FR, DE, IT (UI + voices + vocabularies).
- Sold worldwide via App Store; reimbursement only in US, parts of EU, AU.
