# What it is
Companion app to the Zones of Regulation curriculum (Leah Kuypers' framework, used in OT/SLP/school settings). Four colored zones (blue, green, yellow, red) categorize emotional/alertness states; the app teaches identification and offers calming and regulatory tool selection. Bundle adds Exploring Emotions (entry-level companion).

# Pricing
- Zones of Regulation App — USD 4.99 one-time, App Store (iOS).
- Exploring Emotions App — USD 4.99 one-time.
- Complete Bundle — combined IAP.

# Features
- 4-zone emotion identification game.
- Tool exploration: calming techniques, sensory supports, cognitive strategies.
- Practice scenarios.
- Progress tracking per user (lite).
- Tied to the Zones curriculum used in many US schools.

# Strengths
- Backed by an established curriculum already in classroom use across US/UK/AU.
- Cheap one-time price.
- Direct fit with school OT goals.
- Simple and focused.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- iOS only.
- English only.
- Game-style content; not a daily-use tool — more of a worksheet replacement.
- Requires the curriculum context to be valuable.
- Very dated UI; visibly under-maintained.
- No real-time emotion logging — kid taps a zone in the lesson, not as an in-the-moment regulation prompt.

# Evidence base
- Zones curriculum has classroom adoption literature; the app itself has no RCT.

# Geographic / language coverage
- English only.
- App Store worldwide.
