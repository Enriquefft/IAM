# What it is
Game-based learning app for kids with autism. Library of 100+ mini-games across early literacy, math, tracing, memory, sorting, sound recognition, emotion identification. Mom's Choice Award. Cross-platform.

# Pricing
- 14-day free trial.
- Annual — USD 59.99/year.

# Features
- 100+ activities across reading, math, tracing, memory, matching/sorting, sound/word recognition, emotions.
- Difficulty progression.
- Available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, Indonesian, Turkish.
- iOS and Android.
- No ads.

# Strengths
- Cheaper annual subscription than Otsimo while covering similar ground.
- Five language localizations including Spanish and Portuguese — better LATAM fit than most US-origin apps.
- Cross-platform out of the box.
- Mom's Choice Award gives some institutional credibility.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- "Autism" branding around generic preschool learning games — most activities are not autism-specific in their design.
- No personalization model; just a difficulty knob.
- Spanish localization quality has not been independently audited.
- No data export, no clinician access.
- No AAC, no schedule, no behavioral support.
- Single-developer / small team; release cadence is slow.
- Discoverable mostly via app store ASO; limited credibility outside app store reviews.

# Evidence base
- No peer-reviewed trials.

# Geographic / language coverage
- 5 UI/content languages.
- App Store and Google Play worldwide.
