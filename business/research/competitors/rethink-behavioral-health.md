# What it is
ABA and pediatric-therapy practice-management platform. Bundles clinical programming, data collection, AI scheduling, session docs, billing, RCM, RBT training, parent portal. Distinguishes itself with a 1,500+ program library and parent-facing engagement tools.

# Pricing
- Contact for pricing. Quoted by user count, modules, services.
- `Estimate:` typical clinic spend USD 60-100/user/month based on third-party reviews.

# Features
- Clinical programming, ABA data collection.
- AI-powered scheduling.
- Session documentation.
- Revenue cycle management.
- Staff training (BACB-criteria RBT videos).
- Family engagement portal.
- Mobile data collection app with offline.
- 1,500+ customizable evidence-based programs.
- Automated claim scrubbing.

# Strengths
- Long-running player; serves school districts as well as clinics.
- Family engagement portal is more substantive than CentralReach's.
- Pre-built program library reduces clinical-design overhead.
- BACB-criteria RBT training included.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- US only.
- Pricing opacity.
- ABA / behavioral focus; lighter on AAC and OT.
- No Spanish UI documented.
- No direct kid-facing app — parent and clinician only.
- The "family engagement" piece is portal-style, not an actual parent app the parent would use voluntarily.

# Evidence base
- Tooling, not clinical claim.

# Geographic / language coverage
- English, US.
