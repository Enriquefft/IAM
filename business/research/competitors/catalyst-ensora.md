# What it is
ABA data collection tool from Therapy Brands (rebranded as Ensora). Mobile-first: an RBT or BCBA goes into a session, takes a tablet, charts trials/behaviors as they happen, app generates graphs automatically. Often paired with separate practice-management EHRs.

# Pricing
- Range USD 100-500/month depending on scale (per third-party reviews).
- Pricing not publicly listed; contact-for-pricing model.

# Features
- DTT, duration, frequency, rate, task analysis, quantity, anecdotal data.
- Real-time data entry on tablet.
- Auto-generated graphs.
- Templates for common programs.
- Offline data entry, sync later.
- Treatment plan creation, behavior reduction strategies.

# Strengths
- Therapist-friendly mobile-first UX — the data-entry experience is among the cleanest in ABA.
- Strong fit for clinics where staff travel to homes/schools.
- Cheaper than CentralReach for small practices.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- Not full practice management — needs an external EHR or billing.
- US/Canada only.
- English only.
- No parent / kid product.
- ABA-only; not used by SLPs / OTs.
- Pricing opacity is a real friction.

# Evidence base
- Compliance tool, not a clinical claim.

# Geographic / language coverage
- English, US/CA.
