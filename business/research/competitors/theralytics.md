# What it is
ABA practice-management platform. Sub-USD 20/user starting price, ONC-certified and SOC 2 Type II compliant — among the cheapest serious ABA software options for small US clinics.

# Pricing
- From USD 15/user/month.
- ONC-certified and SOC 2 Type II compliant.
- Pricing structure being held through May 31, 2026 per public commitment.

# Features
- Scheduling, drag-drop, drive-time, mileage tracking.
- Billing: claims, denial management, A/R recovery.
- Data collection + analytics.
- Generative AI for session-note narrative summaries.
- Document management.
- HIPAA-compliant.

# Strengths
- Cheapest credible ABA platform in the US market.
- ONC + SOC 2 certifications are real differentiators.
- Active price-reduction stance (publicly committing to not raising prices).
- AI session notes feature parity with bigger players.

# Weaknesses / gaps
- US only.
- ABA-only.
- English only.
- Smallest install base of the named competitors.
- No direct kid or parent product.
- No LATAM presence.

- Tooling.
