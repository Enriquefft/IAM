# Question
Can Project ImPACT (Improving Parents As Communication Teachers), a parent-mediated NDBI for community settings, increase parent intervention fidelity and child spontaneous language?

# Method
- Single-subject multiple-baseline design, 8 dyads.
- Outcomes: parent fidelity in technique use; child spontaneous language.
- Multilevel modeling for within-session relationships.

# Findings
- All parents increased their use of intervention techniques.
- 6 of 8 children showed improvements in spontaneous language.
- Significant association between parent strategy use and child spontaneous language.

# Limitations
- Single-subject pilot, no control group.
- Small sample (n=8).
- No long-term follow-up.
