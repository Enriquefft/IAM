# Question
What is the FDA-cleared scope of the EarliPoint Evaluation eye-tracking device for autism diagnosis?

# Method
- 510(k) clearance documentation, June 2023.

# Findings
- FDA cleared as objective tool to aid in diagnosis and assessment of autism in children 16-30 months in specialty centers.
- Built on Tobii eye-tracking hardware.
- 12-minute assessment.
- 120 Hz eye-tracking sampling.
- Backed by Jones 2023 JAMA pivotal trial (n=475, sens 71-78%, spec 80.7-85.4%).

# Limitations
- Limited to specialty-center deployment per labeling.
- US-only commercial availability.
- Pricing not public.
