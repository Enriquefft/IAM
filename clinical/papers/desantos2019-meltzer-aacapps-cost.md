# Question
What are current price points and funding pathways for dedicated AAC devices vs iPad-based AAC?

# Method
- Practitioner-maintained reference list updated 2025-10-23.
- Aggregates manufacturer pricing, US Medicaid funding eligibility, and software inclusion.

# Findings
- Dedicated SGDs (Tobii Dynavox I-13/I-16, PRC-Saltillo Accent): USD ~$4,000–$12,000.
- Tablet-based with locked AAC app: USD ~$800–$2,500.
- Software-only AAC subscription: USD ~$99–$499/year.
- US Medicare/Medicaid funding requires "dedicated" classification (locked from non-AAC use).
- Tobii Dynavox global market share ~25-28%; PRC-Saltillo ~12-15%.

# Builder takeaways
- Dedicated SGD market is funded by US insurance — that's the entire economic model. LATAM lacks equivalent structure (no Medicaid analog covers SGDs in PE/EC/CO; some MX coverage; ARG/CHL via discapacidad).
- iPad + locked app is the realistic LATAM pathway. Proloquo2Go ($249.99 one-time), TouchChat ($299.99), TD Snap (subscription).
- Wedge: a Spanish/Quechua-localized AAC app with offline mode and SLP-collaboration features is structurally absent in LATAM. ARASAAC has the symbol library but no production AAC app.
