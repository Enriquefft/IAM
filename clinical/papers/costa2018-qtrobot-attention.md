# Question
Does interaction with QTrobot increase attention and reduce repetitive behaviors in children with autism vs interaction with a human?

# Method
- Within-subject comparison: QTrobot vs human interaction conditions.
- Outcomes: attention, imitation, repetitive/stereotyped behaviors via video coding.

# Findings
- Significant positive results across attention, imitation, and reduction in stereotyped behaviors during QTrobot condition.
- QTrobot proved engaging in laboratory setting.

# Effect sizes
| Outcome | Measure | Effect | 95% CI | p |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Attention | observation | significant increase | n/r | <0.05 |
| Repetitive behavior | observation | significant decrease | n/r | <0.05 |

# Limitations
- Small sample.
- Single laboratory setting.
- No follow-up beyond session.

None.
