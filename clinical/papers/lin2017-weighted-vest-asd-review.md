# Question
Are weighted vests effective for behavioral, attentional or sensory outcomes in individuals with ASD?

# Method
- Systematic review of 32 studies; What Works Clearinghouse standards applied.
- 4 (12%) "meets standards"; 6 (19%) "meets standards with reservations".

# Findings
- Among single-subject studies applying a weighted vest, only 1/7 demonstrated positive effects.
- Weighted vests "ineffective in remediating behaviour problems of children with autism spectrum disorders".
- Not classified as evidence-based practice for ASD.

# Limitations
- Most included studies underpowered single-subject designs.
- Heterogeneous outcome definitions.

# Quotes
> "weighted vests for students with autism spectrum disorder is not an evidence-based practice"
