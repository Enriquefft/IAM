# Status

Research file, not legal advice. Ecuadorian counsel required before launch. The 2021 LOPDP is recent and Reglamento was issued 2023; SaMD-specific guidance from ARCSA is partial. Several open questions on enforcement practice.

# Data protection

## Primary law
- **Ley Orgánica de Protección de Datos Personales** (LOPDP). Approved by Asamblea Nacional 2021-05-10; published Registro Oficial 2021-05-26. <https://www.telecomunicaciones.gob.ec/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/Ley-Organica-de-Datos-Personales.pdf>.
- **Reglamento General LOPDP** — Decreto Ejecutivo 904, 2023-11-13. <https://www.telecomunicaciones.gob.ec/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Decreto-Ejecutivo-No.-904.pdf>.
- Authority: **Superintendencia de Protección de Datos Personales** (SPDP). Created by LOPDP. Sanctioning powers entered into force 2023-05-26 after the 2-year transition.

## Sensitive data and health
- LOPDP art. 4(20) — `datos sensibles` includes ethnicity, gender identity, religion, ideology, political affiliation, criminal history, migration status, sexual orientation, **health, biometric, genetic data**.
- LOPDP art. 26 — processing sensitive data is prohibited except: explicit consent, vital interests, public interest established by law, processing by foundation/association related to its purpose, or judicial proceedings. Strict regime.
- Health data — LOPDP art. 28 specifically: treatment must comply with minimum parameters in the law and SPDP regulations.

## Children and minors — Ecuador's two-tier consent
- LOPDP art. 22:
  - **Under 15:** treatment requires explicit consent of the legal representative.
  - **15 and over:** the adolescent may give explicit consent directly.
- Sensitive-data and automated-decision-based processing of minors' data **always** requires legal representative consent (regardless of age).
- Controller must provide information in clear, simple, age-appropriate language so the minor can understand purposes, rights, and exercise mechanisms.

## Penalties
- LOPDP art. 67–69: leves up to 1–10 SBU; graves 10–20 SBU; muy graves up to 0.7%–1% of global turnover (with floor in SBU). Source: Reglamento + LOPDP.
- (1 SBU 2025 = USD 470.) Maximum exposure for very-serious infractions tied to global turnover.

# Medical device classification

## Authority and primary instruments
- **ARCSA** — Agencia Nacional de Regulación, Control y Vigilancia Sanitaria.
- **Resolución ARCSA-DE-026-2016-YMIH** — Normativa Técnica Sanitaria Sustitutiva para el registro sanitario y control de dispositivos médicos de uso humano. Published Registro Oficial Suplemento 921, 2017-01-12. <https://www.controlsanitario.gob.ec/wp-content/uploads/downloads/2017/01/Resoluci%C3%B3n_ARCSA-026-YMIH-Dispositivos-M%C3%A9dicos.pdf>.
- **Resolución ARCSA-DE-2023-033-AKRG** — partial reform 2023-11. <https://www.controlsanitario.gob.ec/wp-content/uploads/downloads/2023/11/Resolucion_ARCSA-DE-2023-033-AKRG_reforma-a-la-Normativa-Tecnica-Sanitaria-para-el-registro-sanitario-de-Dispositivos-medicos-de-uso-humano-y-de-los-establecimientos-en-donde-se-fabrican.pdf>.
- Resolución ARCSA-DE-2023-005-AKRG — notifications regime for medical devices.
- Draft 2025 substitute for 026-2016 in public consultation: <https://www.controlsanitario.gob.ec/wp-content/uploads/downloads/2025/09/Proyecto-borrador-reforma-sustitutiva-a-la-Resolucion-ARCSA-DE-026-2016-YMIH.pdf>. Open question on entry into force.

## Risk classes
- ARCSA risk-based classification: I, II, III, IV (Latin American IMDRF-aligned scheme).

## Software as a Medical Device
- Per Resolución ARCSA-DE-026-2016: software for medical devices is included in the sanitary registration of the medical device for which it was designed.
- Open question: ARCSA's treatment of standalone software (true SaMD with no associated hardware) is not explicitly elaborated in publicly available text — practice tends to follow IMDRF principles by analogy. The 2025 draft substitute is expected to clarify; status as of 2026-05 is `Open question`.

## Wellness / education boundary in Ecuador
The line follows manufacturer's intended purpose. Ecuador has no published wellness safe-harbor.

### Disqualifying claims
- "Diagnostica TEA" — dispositivo médico.
- "Trata el autismo" — dispositivo médico.
- "Detecta crisis sensoriales con biosignales" — dispositivo médico.
- "Reduce síntomas del TEA" — dispositivo médico.
- Screening/tamizaje claims — dispositivo médico.

### Likely-wellness claims
- "Apoyo educativo y comunicación" — wellness/education.
- "Documentación para reflexión personal del cuidador" — wellness.
- "Apoyo a las rutinas" — wellness/education.

### Boundary cases
- Recommending professional consultation — likely permissible if framed generally (Open question).

# Disability and autism — substantive rights framework

## Constitución 2008
- Art. 47 — personas con discapacidad: derecho a atención integral, educación inclusiva, accesibilidad.
- Art. 48 — políticas afirmativas para discapacidad.

## Ley Orgánica de Discapacidades (LOD)
- Published Registro Oficial 796, 2012-09-25.
- CONADIS Ecuador — Consejo Nacional para la Igualdad de Discapacidades.
- Art. 64–65 — accesibilidad de la comunicación, TIC.

## Autism-specific
- **No dedicated TEA national law** equivalent to PE/MX/CL/AR. Coverage operates through the LOD framework.
- Acuerdos Ministeriales del MSP for clinical guidance on TEA exist but do not constitute a comprehensive autism-rights statute.
- Open question: legislative proposals for a Ley TEA Ecuador — status as of 2026-05.

## Consent and minors
- Código de la Niñez y Adolescencia — minors act through parents/guardians; adolescents have evolving capacity (interés superior + autonomía progresiva).
- For data: LOPDP art. 22 (15-year threshold; sensitive data always parental).

# Accessibility / education

- LOD art. 64–65 — TIC accessibility for State + commercial providers offering essential services.
- NTE INEN-ISO/IEC 40500 — Ecuadorian Technical Standard adopting WCAG 2.0. Voluntary unless cited in procurement.
- INEN technical standards on accessibility (NTE INEN 2854, 2855, etc.) — physical and digital accessibility.
- Education: Ministerio de Educación — Sistema Nacional de Inclusión.

# Marketing & advertising claims

## Authority
- ARCSA — health-product advertising under Ley Orgánica de Salud + Reglamento.
- DEFENSORÍA DEL PUEBLO + SEPS / Ministerio de Producción / Comercio Exterior — consumer protection coordination.
- Ley Orgánica de Defensa del Consumidor (LODC) — Codificación 2024.

## Rules
- LODC art. 6, 7 — derecho a información veraz; prohibición de publicidad engañosa.
- Health-product advertising of unregistered products carries sanitary infraction.
- Children-targeted: Código de la Niñez y Adolescencia — Art. 50 — protection from advertising harmful to development.

## Concrete rules for the autism wellness/education app
- No diagnostic/therapeutic/screening claims about TEA without ARCSA registration.
- Verifiable parental consent for child-image use.
- Per LOPDP art. 22, sensitive data of minors always requires legal representative — even for 15+ adolescents.
